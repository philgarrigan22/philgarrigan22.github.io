Pain in the Task

I’ve always been someone that’s written down, in some form, my to-do’s and tasks that need to get done. But let's be honest, nobody actually enjoys doing 90% of the things that we put on a task list. However, that's no reason to be unorganized! Pain in the Task aims to help keep users organized by tracking tasks and to-do's in an effective and visually appealing manner. Tasks can be created, modified, renamed, or deleted and saved instantly. The project is equal parts my drive to stay on task and keep things organized and my obsession of a clean and polished user experience. Pain in the task is a full-stack single page application, utilizing JavaScript, Ruby, A Ruby on Rails back-end API, and MaterializeCSS, among others.
